Origin Story

How PixelPair
Was Born

We met on our first day of Computer Science class and immediately bonded over a mutual obsession: making things on the internet. While other students debated algorithms for fun, we were staying late building side projects.

By our second year, we were already freelancing — landing our first real client through a cold email that somehow worked. We built their website over a weekend. They paid us $150. We thought we were millionaires.

Fast forward two years from graduation — we've delivered over 20 projects, worked with clients across 8 countries, and built a small but mighty reputation for shipping fast, building clean, and never disappearing on a deadline.

PixelPair isn't just a name. It's our philosophy: two minds are better than one. Every project gets both of us — one obsessing over pixels and polish, the other obsessing over architecture and performance.
