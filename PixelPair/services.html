FAQ

How long does a typical project take?+
Most projects take 2–6 weeks depending on scope. Landing pages are typically 1–2 weeks, while full web apps can be 4–8 weeks. We'll give you a detailed timeline during the discovery phase.
Do you work with clients outside India?+
Absolutely — we work with clients worldwide. Our team communicates clearly in English and adapts to different time zones. We've worked with clients across India, Europe, and North America.
What's your payment structure?+
We typically work with 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. For longer projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. We accept UPI, bank transfer, and international wire.
Do you provide ongoing maintenance?+
Yes — all projects include 30 days of post-launch support. We also offer monthly maintenance retainers for ongoing updates, security patches, and feature additions.
Can I see your work before hiring?+
Of course. Check out our portfolio page for detailed case studies. We're also happy to share private references on request.
